Angular and Observables

Reactive programming is about conceptualising our application as streams and operations that are performed on those streams. Observables are a blueprint for creating streams and plumbing them together with operators to create observable chains. RxJS is a library that lets us create and work with observables. Angular has a few public facing APIs that use Observables, the EventEmitter, Forms and HTTP.
